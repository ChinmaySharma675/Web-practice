Academic Programs

  • Engineering: Offering degrees in Electrical, Mechanical, and Computer Engineering.
  • Computer Science: Specializations in Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Software Development.
  • Environmental Science: Interdisciplinary program focused on sustainable energy and conservation.

  • Robotics Club: Design, build, and compete with cutting-edge robots.
  • Coding Workshops: Develop programming skills and create innovative applications.
  • Sustainability Initiatives: Collaborate on green projects to promote environmental stewardship.

Our faculty are recognized leaders in their fields, conducting groundbreaking research at the forefront of innovation. They are dedicated mentors guiding students to achieve their full potential in a dynamic, interdisciplinary environment.

Student Life

  • Diverse Community: Connect with peers from all backgrounds and cultures.
  • Supportive Resources: Access academic advising, career services, and mental health support.
